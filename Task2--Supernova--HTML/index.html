What is Supernova : 


                SuperNova is a start up with two goals to achieve:  

                First, we want to adopt students and be involved in anything
                they want, such as courses, workshops, scholarships, trips, 
                job opportunities, study, contests, even personal problems, 
                events, graduation projects, travel, etc.

                Second, we want to upgrade the thinking of the next generation
                and change their interests from things that do not make sense
                to things really useful and break the routine of education
                and boring traditional education through the dissemination
                of science in a way that is interesting to be different 
               and creative and the student does not feel compelled to do 
               so, and feel that he enjoyed and through it make friends New
                and works who he wants.
